🧩 Port Match

Match every port number on the left with its correct Protocol / TCP-UDP / Use card on the right.
How to play
  1. Click a port number card on the left to select it.
  2. Then click the card on the right that lists its correct Protocol, TCP/UDP, and Use.
  3. A correct pair locks in green and stays visible; a wrong pair flashes red and unselects.
  4. Match every pair with as few moves as possible, in the fastest time.
