Match every insecure, legacy protocol on the left with its encrypted replacement on the right — straight out of the Security+ secure-vs-insecure pairs list.
How to play
  1. Click an insecure protocol card on the left to select it.
  2. Then click the card on the right with its correct secure replacement (Port, Protocol, and Use).
  3. A correct pair locks in green and stays visible; a wrong pair flashes red and unselects.
  4. Match all 6 pairs with as few moves as possible, in the fastest time.
